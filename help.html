How to Use Dits & Dahs?
Do nothing
Make the blink counter
between 1-3 counts to
do nothing
Dits!
Make the blink counter
between 3-6 counts to
make a dits sign
Dahs!
Make the blink counter
between 6-9 counts to
make a dahs sign
GO!
Make the blink counter
10 or more counts as
the enter button
Wave!
Wave your hands on
camera to reset
all progress saved
Blinking?
The border shows red
as not blinking and
green as blinking
